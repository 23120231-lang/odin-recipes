Arugula and Hummus Mini Pizzas

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons hummus, or to taste
  • 1 naan bread
  • 1 cup arugula, or to taste
  • 1 date, pitted and finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and slightly crushed
  • 2 teaspoons pumpkin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar, or to taste

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Spread hummus onto naan bread; top with arugula, date, and pumpkin seeds.
  3. Drizzle balsamic vinegar over pizza.
