Easy Beef Tacos

Description

Quick, simple, and incredibly flavorful. These classic hard-shell ground beef tacos are perfect for a fast weeknight meal that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 8 hard taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 diced tomato

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat until completely cooked, draining any excess fat.
  2. Add the chili powder, cumin, onion powder, and a splash of water. Simmer for 5 minutes until the liquid reduces.
  3. Warm taco shells in the oven according to package instructions.
  4. Assemble the tacos by layering the meat, cheese, lettuce, and tomatoes into the shells.
