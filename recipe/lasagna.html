World's Best Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is packed with layers of savory meat sauce, rich ricotta cheese filling, and melted mozzarella. It takes some time to prepare, but it is the ultimate comfort food that tastes even better the next day.

Perfect for family gatherings, meal prep, or cozy weekend dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. In a large Dutch oven or pot, cook the sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and tomato sauce. Season with parsley, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 1.5 hours.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  4. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread one-half of the ricotta cheese mixture over noodles. Top with a third of mozzarella cheese slices.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 50 minutes. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
