Classic Potato Salad

Description

This is the definitive backyard barbecue side dish. Creamy, slightly tangy, and textured with crisp celery and hard-boiled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes, cubed
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons yellow mustard
  • 3 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup chopped pickles
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add cubed potatoes and cook until fork-tender, about 10-15 minutes. Drain and cool completely.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, mustard, salt, and pepper.
  3. Gently fold in the cooled potatoes, chopped eggs, celery, and pickles until evenly coated.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to let the flavors blend.
